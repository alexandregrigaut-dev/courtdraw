    Features

    Everything a Coach Needs. Nothing They Don't.

    Purpose-built for coaching, not graphic design. Fast, focused, and frustration-free.

    🏟️

    38+ Regulation Courts

    Every court drawn to correct proportions — full pitch, half-court, or zoomed penalty area. Switch sport in one tap.

    ✏️

    9 Drawing Tools

    Solid arrows for runs, dashed arrows for off-ball movement, curve left & right arcs, lines, circles, rectangles, text labels, and an eraser — everything you need for any tactical scenario.

    🧑‍🤝‍🧑

    Draggable Player Tokens

    Sport-specific player counts loaded automatically — 11v11 for football, 5v5 for basketball, 6v6 for volleyball, and so on. Add or remove players, drag to any position, and tap a token to rename it. Works with touch and mouse equally well.

    📚

    Tactic Library

    Save any board state — court, players, drawings — as a named tactic. Load it back in one click. Stored locally, no cloud needed.

    📒

    Personal Playbook PRO

    Turn your saved plays into a structured playbook. Add coach notes, tag plays by situation ("set play", "press defense", "U14 session"), rate 1–5 stars, and search your library in seconds.

    📅

    Training Sessions PRO

    Chain 3–8 saved plays into a full training plan. Set drill durations, step through each play on the pitch, and export the whole session as a multi-page PDF — ready to hand to your assistant coaches.

    🖼️

    PNG Export

    Export a high-res 1200×720 PNG of your tactic. Share directly in WhatsApp, Telegram, or paste into a presentation.

    ↩️

    50-Step Undo / Redo

    Full 50-step undo history. Fix a misplaced arrow or undo an accidental clear without losing your whole board.

    📶

    Works Offline

    CourtDraw is a Progressive Web App. Install it to your home screen and draw plays pitch-side with zero signal required.

    🌙

    Dark & Light Mode

    Dark interface by default for pitch-side use. Flip to light mode for presentations on a projector or printed handouts.

    ▶️

    Animated Playback PRO

    Hit Play and watch your tactical sequence unfold automatically — phase by phase, at your pace. Perfect for team talks and video analysis sessions.

    📹

    Video Overlay PRO

    Upload match footage and draw plays directly on top, frame by frame. Annotate real match situations just like Hudl or Wyscout — now built right into your tactics board.

    🗂️

    200+ Ready-Made Plays PRO

    Start from a library of 200+ pre-built tactics — formations, set pieces, and plays across 12 sports including football, basketball, ice hockey, rugby, and more. Load and modify in one click.

    📺

    Club Presentation Mode CLUB

    One tap to go full-screen. Toolbar and header are replaced by a clean overlay showing your club logo, tactic name, and phase controls — projector-ready for team meetings, parent evenings, and scouting sessions.

    💬

    Coach Notes on Tactics CLUB

    Every play in the club library can carry coach notes — situational tips, when to use it, what it counters. Your shared library becomes a living coaching document, not just a folder of diagrams.

    📱

    Touch-First on Mobile

    Pinch to zoom, two-finger pan, collapsible toolbar, and an immersive landscape mode that hides all chrome so the court fills your screen. Buttery-smooth drawing on iPad and Android tablets.

    Quick Start

    From Zero to Tactical Masterplan in Under 60 Seconds

    No tutorial required. Pick a court and start drawing — it's that simple.

    1

    Choose Your Sport

    Tap any of the 38+ courts — football pitch, basketball half-court, padel, hockey rink, and more.

    2

    Place Your Players

    Sport-specific positions are pre-placed automatically. Drag any token to adjust, tap to rename, long-press to remove.

    3

    Draw the Play

    Pick a colour and tool — arrow for runs, dashed for off-ball, arc for crosses, text for cues.

    4

    Save & Share

    Save to your tactic library or export a high-res PNG and drop it straight into the team group chat.
